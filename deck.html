METAL DECK SHEET

DECK SHEET

Composite Deck sheet is a steel profile with ribs, embossments which helps in binding with concrete slab and together forms a part of the floor structure. This interlocking between the concrete and the floor deck creates a reinforced concrete slab that serves the dual purpose of permanent shuttering and reinforcement. Nowadays Composite decking has become the most effective method constructing floors in steel buildings and very useful in high rise Buildings.

FEATURES

Technical Specifications

Decks are available in 44 mm and 52 mm depth profiles with thickness range of 0.63 mm to 2 mm

Length:Up to 12000 mm

Material:CR Steel,HR Steel,Galvanized,Colour Coated

Type:Bare,Primer Coated,Galvanized,Pre-Painted

Yield Strength:240 Mpa to 350 Mpa (550 Mpa available in Tuffdeck 44)

We Supply Suitable size of decking sheets,as per customer`s specifications,drawings,for easy erection and also to avoid any wastage,rework at site.

Benefits of Tuffdeck

  • Reduces concrete and slab thickness due to light weight
  • Acts as a composite member and as permanent shuttering
  • It can be used as working platform during construction
  • Reduces the construction time and total project cost
  • Light weight and do not require any support
  • No major reinforcement required.
  • Metal deck can also be used as roofing and cladding sheet in cement,power plants and bulk material handling plants.
