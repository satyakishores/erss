About Us

Corporate Profile

Engineers Roofing Solutionsis engaged in designing and manufacturing of superior quality Prefabricated Building Products, Engineers Roofing Solutions has maintained providing fine quality products with remarkable technical support and guidance to their concerned clients. Affordable in price, roofing systems give a healthy and calm environment to any building. Strong base, good ventilation are the core aspects for any building that requires in living or working atmosphere. Roof is nothing but safety at any atmosphere. Various roofing and designs are preferred as per the needs.

We specialize in different types of roofing like warehouse roofing, arch roofing, corrugated roofing panels, corrugated roofing, corrugated steel roofing sheets, curved roofing systems, curved roof, Galvalume sheet roofing.

We manufacture all kinds of building materials such as Pre-engineered building systems, Color coated roofing sheets, Pre-Painted Galvanized Steel sheets, Aluminum Zinc Alloy Coated Steel sheets, Roof top ventilation systems, Self-tapping screws, etc.
