PEB Structural

Roofing is a special design profile for stronger roofing application. Its optimum pitch and depth ratio lends superior weight bearing capacity,make it the profile of choice.

Its unique ratio lends is made for a water proof roofing solution with the added advantage of an anti capillary groove,which ensures a leak proof installation for years to come.

The design also prevents moisture up on fasteners preventing premature corrosion.
